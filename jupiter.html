Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, known for its massive size, colorful cloud bands, and iconic Great Red Spot—a giant storm raging for centuries. It has 79 moons, including the four largest, the Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto. Jupiter is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium, with no solid surface.

Some Fun Facts about Jupiter

  • Jupiter has the fastest rotation in the solar system, completing a day in just about 10 hours!
  • It's so huge that over 1,300 Earth could fit inside, and it's twice as massive as all other planets combined.
  • This giant storm is bigger than Earth and has been raging for for at least 350 years.
  • Jupiter has 79 moons, with four big ones--lo,Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto--- discovered by Gallieo in 1610.
  • If you weigh 50 kg on Earth, you’d weigh about 130 kg on Jupiter due to its intense gravity.
