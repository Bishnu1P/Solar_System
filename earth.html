Earth, our vibrant home planet, is the third from the Sun and the only known world to support life. It features breathtaking landscapes, vast oceans covering 71% of its surface, and a life-sustaining atmosphere. With its dynamic climate, rich biodiversity, and protective magnetic field, Earth is a unique and thriving oasis in the vastness of space.

Some Fun Facts about Earth

  • Earth spins at about 1,670 km/h (1,037 mph) at the equator, making a full rotation every 24 hours.
  • Earth and Venus are often called twins because they're similar in size and structure, but Venus is way hotter and inhospitable.
  • Around 71% of Earth's surface is covered in water, which is why it looks blue from space.
  • Earth might seem massive, but it could fit over 1.3 million times into the Sun!
  • Earth’s magnetic field acts as a shield, protecting us from harmful solar radiation.
